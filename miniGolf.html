Mini Golf



Instructions

  1. Choose between Multiplayer and Singleplayer and then choose how many holes you want to play
  2. Drag your mouse back to determime how fast you want to hit the ball and where you want to aim
  3. Make it to the hole in the fewest amount of strokes possible!
  4. Orange Circles make your ball bounce further and dark green and light green boxes push your ball the way the arrow is facing
